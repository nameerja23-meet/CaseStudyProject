    Our mission is to improve

    the lives of the millions that

    suffer from food allergies

    and gluten-related disorders

    FIND OUT HOW

     

    Food allergies and gluten-related disorders are on the rise,

    with significant social, health, and economic impact

    $25B

    each year is spent on healthcare costs related to food allergies and disorders

    1 in every 13 children in

    the United States is affected by food allergies

    new cases of celiac

    disease have doubled every fifteen years

     We are using cutting

    edge technologies in immunology, computational biology, and protein engineering to develop a new approach 

    We are building a comprehensive, clinically validated map of the molecular structure of food allergies and disorders, unlocking critical knowledge about the biological mechanisms that drive human food interactions.

    On the basis of this map, we design proteins that do not trigger an immune response. These proteins are then applied to the food and therapeutics markets.
